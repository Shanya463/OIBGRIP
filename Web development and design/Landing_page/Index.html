TITAN

Titan Company Limited (earlier known as Titan Industries Limited) is an Indian luxury products company that mainly manufactures fashion accessories such as jewellery, watches and eyewear. It is the world's fifth largest wrist watch manufacturer and India's leading producer of watches. Part of the Tata Group and started as a joint venture with TIDCO, the company has its corporate headquarters in Electronic City, Bangalore, and registered office in Hosur, Tamil Nadu. Also, Titan announced recently (during the last quarter of 2016) about launching a series of affordable smartwatches under its brands like Sonata and Fastrack soon.
